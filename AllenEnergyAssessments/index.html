Residential Building Energy Rating (BER)

A BER assessment provides a clear, independent measure of your property’s energy performance. Our service delivers a fully compliant BER carried out by a registered SEAI assessor.

We survey the building fabric, insulation levels, ventilation, heating systems, and renewable technologies to generate an accurate BER rating. Alongside the official certificate and advisory report, you receive a comprehensive set of recommendations tailored to your property.

SEAI Grant Information

Take advantage of available SEAI grants to upgrade your home's efficiency. Full details are available on the SEAI Website.

Insulation Grants

  • • Grants available for attic, cavity wall, internal wall, and external wall insulation.
  • • Increased grant amounts for attic and cavity insulation.
  • • Higher fixed grants for first-time buyers and households on qualifying welfare payments.
  • • New: Homeowners who previously used a wall-insulation grant can now apply for a second wall-insulation measure.

Heat Pump & Heating Grants

  • • Grants available for a range of heat pump systems (air-to-water, ground source, etc.).
  • • Increased heat pump grant amounts in recent updates.
  • • Grant support available for the required heat pump technical assessment.
  • • Heating system upgrade grant available (e.g., add zoned heating to separate rads/hot water).

Renewable Energy Grants

  • • Solar PV grants available to generate your own electricity.
  • • Solar thermal grants available for upgrading hot water systems.

One-Stop-Shop (Full Retrofit)

  • • Grants for full home energy upgrades aiming for a B2 BER or better.
  • • Covers insulation, heat pumps, ventilation, windows/doors, and solar PV.
  • • Includes project management and BERs through registered providers.
  • • Fully funded upgrades available for eligible households on qualifying welfare payments.

Additional Eligibility Notes: Grants generally apply to homes built before specific cut-off years (varies by measure). Some grants are available to local authorities and approved housing bodies.

SEAI Windows and Doors Grant

SEAI recently announced a positive change to the window and doors grant where homeowners can now get grants for individual doors and windows without carrying out a deep retrofit to their home.

Grant Payment Criteria

  • A post-works BER is required after installation (this is grant-aided). We recommend our combined pre and post-works BER service.
  • New windows/doors must meet required U-values (1.4 W/m²K, or better), confirmed on the declaration of works.
  • Property must meet a Heat Loss Indicator (HLI) value of 2.3 W/K·m² or better OR attic and walls must be noted as "Good" or "Very Good". (We check this prior to installation).

Grant Values Available

Window Replacements

  • Apartment: €1,500
  • Mid-Terrace: €1,800
  • Semi-Detached / End of Terrace: €3,000
  • Detached: €4,000

External Doors

Per Door (Max of 2 doors): €800
