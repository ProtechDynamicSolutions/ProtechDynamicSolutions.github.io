Unlock SEAI Home Energy Grants

The Sustainable Energy Authority of Ireland (SEAI) offers substantial grants to help homeowners upgrade their properties. As registered professionals, we guide you through the technical requirements to secure your funding.

  • Grants up to €30,000+ available for deep retrofits.
  • Funding for Insulation, Heat Pumps, and Solar PV.
  • A professional Technical Assessment is required before starting works.
