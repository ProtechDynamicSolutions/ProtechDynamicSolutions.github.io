Industrial & Commercial Mechanical Services Ltd. (ICMS) was founded by John Hurley and Finbarr O'Shea, who bring over 25 years of individual experience in the Mechanical Contracting Industry. This positions our firm not as a new entrant, but as an expert-led specialist vehicle leveraging decades of combined knowledge.

"Our core value is the ability to deliver the most complex of projects to the highest standards of quality while doing so on schedule, within budget, and at all times with safety to the forefront."

We are a specialist B2B mechanical engineering sub-contractor, trusted by main contractors to deliver critical mechanical packages for major industrial and commercial builds.

Our Core Services

High-Specification Solutions for Industry

We specialize in the design, fabrication, and installation of complex mechanical systems tailored for demanding environments, including industrial, commercial, and sustainable installations.

HVAC Systems

Design, installation, and commissioning of high-efficiency heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems.

Plumbing & Process Piping

Expert installation of industrial-grade plumbing and complex process piping for manufacturing and tech.

Medical & Processed Gases

Certified installation of high-purity gas systems for medical-device, pharmaceutical, and laboratory environments.

Water Treatment Systems

Implementation of specialized systems including Reverse Osmosis (RO), high-purity water, and waste water treatment.

Steam Systems

Installation and maintenance of industrial steam and condensate systems for manufacturing and processing.

Sustainable Installations

Integrating modern, sustainable technologies and renewable energy solutions into mechanical systems.

A Critical Operational Differentiator

Our In-House Fabrication Workshop

Our facility at Airport East Business Park is not just an office. It houses our state-of-the-art fabrication, workshop, and storage facility. This is a key strategic asset that sets us apart.

Superior Quality Control

Manufacturing components like pipe spools and modular skids in a controlled workshop environment is far more precise than on-site fabrication.

Safety & Minimized Disruption

Off-site pre-fabrication significantly reduces "hot work" (welding, grinding) in sensitive client environments like clean-rooms or active facilities.

Project Acceleration

We build components in parallel with on-site civil work, compressing project timelines and ensuring on-time delivery.
