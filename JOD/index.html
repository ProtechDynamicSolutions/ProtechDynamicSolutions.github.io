About JOD Accounting

Who We Are

Based in Macroom, Co. Cork, John O'Donoghue Accounting Solutions Limited is an agile micro-company dedicated to delivering highly personalised financial services. Over the past 2 years, we have built a reputation for accuracy, integrity, and proactive financial management.

Our Philosophy

We believe that sound financial management is the bedrock of any successful enterprise. We work closely with our clients, transforming complex tax and auditing requirements into clear, actionable strategies that protect assets and promote sustainable growth in Euros.

Principal Activities

Expertise & Services

Comprehensive accounting and tax solutions tailored to safeguard your business and optimise your financial performance.

Accounting & Bookkeeping

Meticulous day-to-day bookkeeping and preparation of annual accounts. We ensure your financial records are accurate, up-to-date, and fully compliant with Irish regulatory standards.

Tax Consultancy

Strategic tax planning for individuals and corporations. We help you navigate complex tax legislations to minimise liabilities while ensuring strict compliance with the Revenue Commissioners.

Auditing Activities

Independent and objective auditing services providing assurance to stakeholders regarding the true and fair view of your company's financial statements.

Financial Projections

Robust financial forecasting to support business planning, secure funding, and guide executive decision-making during periods of expansion or restructuring.

Corporate Restructuring

Advisory services tailored to reorganising your company's ownership, operational, or legal structures to improve profitability and streamline operations.

Start-up Support

From company registration (CRO) to setting up initial payroll and VAT systems, we provide the foundational financial support new businesses need to thrive.
