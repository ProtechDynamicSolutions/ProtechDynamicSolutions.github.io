Traversing Munster via helicopter and elite ground transport, tracking the famed coastal route.

Day One

The Aristocrat's Arrival.

Your private jet is met directly on the tarmac at Shannon Airport by our senior Chauffeur-Concierge. Circumventing the main terminal, you will be escorted to a waiting twin-engine luxury helicopter.

A brief, breathtaking flight over the patchwork fields of Munster concludes with a landing on the private lawns of Adare Manor. This evening, enjoy an after-hours, private dining experience curated by a Michelin-starred chef within the closed walls of the estate's oldest chambers.

County Limerick

Day Two

The Locked Vaults.

A morning departure via luxury Range Rover Vogue brings you to the gates of the Old Midleton Distillery. We have secured unprecedented access to the locked maturation rooms—an area strictly unavailable to the public.

Accompanied by the Master Distiller, taste directly from exclusive, decades-old casks of Midleton Very Rare. To conclude the immersion, you will secure your own rare vintage, personalised and sealed on-site for your private cellar back home.

County Cork

Day Three

The Untamed Coast.

Bypass the tourist corridors entirely. Today, your chartered aviation explores the rugged cliffs of the Wild Atlantic Way from above. Soar past the Cliffs of Moher and the Aran Islands before making a secluded landing on a private coastal bluff.

Awaiting you is a private sommelier and a champagne toast overlooking the churning Atlantic, accompanied by fresh native oysters sourced that very morning. The flight continues north, alighting on the grounds of Ashford Castle in time for evening twilight.

The Wild Atlantic Way

Day Four

The Sport of Kings.

The sprawling, closed estate of Ashford Castle is yours to command. The morning is reserved for bespoke equestrian pursuits through the deep, ancient woodlands, mounted on purebred Irish hunters.

In the afternoon, engage in the ancient aristocratic art of falconry, handling magnificent birds of prey under the tutelage of Ireland's foremost Falconer. Conclude with a deeply immersive evening where local historians share the magical ancient folklore of the region beside a roaring peat fire.
