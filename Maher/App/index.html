Quick Reference
AS 3610 · AS 1379 · AS/NZS 4671 · ATO GST Rules
AS 3610 — Formwork for Concrete
Concrete density for design: 2400 kg/m³ normal, 2300 kg/m³ lightweight.
Self-weight of formwork: 0.5–1.0 kPa typical for design loads.
Live load from workers and equipment: minimum 2.4 kPa on formwork.
Minimum formwork pressure: full hydrostatic ρgH, reducible per CIRIA C30.
Exposed formwork surfaces must be sealed against concrete paste leakage.
Check re-propping requirements before stripping upper levels.
Concrete Mixes — AS 1379
N20 — Normal class, general use, 20 MPa at 28 days.
N25 — Reinforced slabs and beams, 25 MPa at 28 days.
N32 — Industrial floors, elevated slabs, 32 MPa.
N40 — High-load columns, transfer structures, 40 MPa.
S class — Special class; specify when non-standard performance is required.
Order 5–10% extra volume to allow for waste, spillage and batching variation.
Rebar Reference — AS/NZS 4671
N-grade: fy = 500 MPa, fu = 650 MPa. Deformed bar, normal ductility.
L-grade: fy = 500 MPa, low ductility. Welded mesh only.
Standard lap length: 40–60 × bar diameter (varies with class and exposure).
Hook allowance: 12d (180° standard hook), 6d (90° hook extension).
Stock lengths: 6 m (N10–N20), 9 m (N20–N28), 12 m (N28–N36).
Tie wire consumption: approximately 5–8 kg per tonne of reinforcement.
Australian GST — ATO Rules
GST registration is mandatory once annual turnover exceeds $75,000. Voluntary registration is permitted below this threshold.
GST rate: 10% on all taxable supplies including construction materials and labour.
An ABN is required to charge and collect GST on invoices.
Input tax credits (ITCs) allow GST-registered businesses to reclaim GST paid on purchases.
A valid tax invoice is required for any taxable supply over $82.50 (incl. GST).
BAS lodgement: quarterly for most businesses, monthly for turnover exceeding $20 million.
If not registered, you must not charge GST and cannot claim input tax credits.
