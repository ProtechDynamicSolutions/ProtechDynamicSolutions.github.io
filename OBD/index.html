Our Studio

About OBD Architecture

OBD Architecture is an innovative design practice based in Cork, Ireland. Led by Principal Designer and Planner Garbhan O'Brien, our studio specialises in crafting bespoke architectural solutions that respect our rich natural landscape while embracing contemporary design principles. Though we operate as a tight-knit, focused team, our collective expertise allows us to deliver projects of exceptional quality and scale.

Garbhan brings a wealth of diverse experience to every commission. With a strong background in the rigorous demands of healthcare design and project management, this precision is seamlessly translated into our residential, commercial, and landscape projects. From crafting intimate family homes and dynamic office spaces to designing the landscapes that surround them, we believe that great architecture is the result of a profound dialogue between the site, the brief, and the client.

Whether overseeing a complex site planning application or managing a custom new build from initial sketch to final handover, our team is dedicated to guiding you through every step of the journey. We blend meticulous project management with visionary design to create spaces that truly resonate.
