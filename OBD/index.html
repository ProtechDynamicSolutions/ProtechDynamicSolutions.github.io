About OBD Architecture

What we do

We work closely with clients to ensure beautifully crafted design that provides natural light and space to promote well-being, whilst effortlessly enhancing flow and connection with the outdoors.

How we do it

Each project is custom designed around the client's needs, taking account of site conditions, budget and environmental factors. We draw upon our vast experience to ensure each client receives honest and informed advice ensuring a design solution that they will love.

What We Do

Expertise & Services

At OBD Architecture, we offer a comprehensive suite of services tailored to bring your vision to life.

Design and Planning

Whether you are looking to simply improve the internal layout of your existing home or build a brand new home from scratch, OBD Architecture can provided tailored design solutions to your make your vision a reality.

Bespoke New Builds

Client focused, considered design solutions that blend seamlessly into their surroundings while maximising site potential.

Extensions and Renovations

As life moves on, so do your requirements of your home. We specialise in optimising and adapting homes to meet the needs of the 'now' whilst also planning for the future.

Landscape Design

OBD Architecture's expertise doesn't stop at the external walls. We provide elegant and natural outdoor spaces that connect thoughtfully with your home and lifestyle.

3D Printed Models

We can provide physical scale models as part of our design process, enabling you to visualise your dream long before works commence on site.

Building and Site Surveys

All successful projects rely on accurate base point data. We provide building and site surveys to ensure an optimal design solution whilst also mitigating the risk of a mishap on site.

Cost Estimates

At OBD Architecture, we recognise that cost is one of the primary drivers of any project. We can provide detailed cost estimates to ensure cost certainty and budget control.
